RELAX & REFRESH

Have your mimosa and your massage too. At The Rise Spa Bar, we offer a variety of alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages to add an extra level of revitalization to your spa day.  Featuring local spirits and Nevada-inspired ingredients, get a tour of the region with the best locally distilled and brewed beverages in the area. Discover a new artisanal cocktail and craft beer, sip back and relax with a sparkling mocktail, or boost your wellness with a health elixir. Whatever your mix, The Rise Bar has something new to elevate your day.
