EXPRESS LOUNGE

Discover Reno's
First-EVER express SPA

‍
We believe the spa shouldn't be reserved as a once-per-year luxury. It's a powerful tool for helping you combat stress and soothe away soreness. The Express Treatment Lounge at The Refuge Spa is designed to remove the barriers and make it easy to fit the spa into your schedule. Stop by on the fly at the end of a long workday to de-stress with a 30 minute chair massage. Get a skin refresh between facials with our Glow & Go facial. Soothe achy soles with our Salty Sole foot scrub. Or, get a quick beauty booster for a night on the town with a Relax & Wax treatment.
