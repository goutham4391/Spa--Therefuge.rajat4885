FULL-SERVICE SPA TREATMENTS

Take a Day Away to Refuge

At The Refuge, you can connect or disconnect, you can get in your comfort zone…and out of it. This is about you - craft your journey and create a spa day that will give you just the right dose of whatever it is you need most right now.

Designed to Be Different

The full-service spa at The Refuge is designed to offer the classic comforts you'd expect from a resort-style spa combined with many you wouldn't. Enjoy an Essential Massage or mix it up with our Warm & Wind Down hot towel massage. Pick a spa Escape package with a classic massage and facial combo or explore something new with our locally-inspired Black Rock Remedy Escape. Whether you're more of a "tried-and-true" type or a "something new" type, either way, you'll be pleasantly surprised at The Refuge.

AMENITIES

LOCKER ROOMS
& SHOWERS

Slip into your swanky robe and sandals and get ready to relax. The private male and female locker rooms at The Refuge come complete with showers, vanity, and amenities to help you dress down for your treatment or dress back up for your dinner plans.

LOUNGE & SAUNA

Be transported to a whole new world in the Reflect Lounge as you admire the beautiful reflective art, cozy into a plush chair, and enjoy complimentary refreshments. Or step into the infrared sauna to warm up and release tension as you detox from the daily grind.

THE RISE SPA BAR

The Rise Bar offers some of the most unique, hand-crafted cocktails, craft beers, and health elixirs designed to elevate your spa experience. Designed with local spirit in mind, The Rise Bar features a variety of local spirits and craft beers that take you on a tour through the city. It's a bar experience as unique as Reno itself.
